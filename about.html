About Our Organization

Our Context & Purpose

The West African agricultural landscape—especially the cocoa sector—faces structural limitations, including lack of data transparency, climate vulnerability, and unfair value distribution for smallholders. Sprouted Roots LBG positions itself as a technical and strategic intermediary, matching rural infrastructure with the uncompromising social and environmental compliance demands of global consumers.

Strategic Guiding Pillars

We approach agricultural transformation through balanced operations structured around institutional capacity building, commercial realignment, and community ecosystem health. Our primary interventions prioritize:

Gender Inclusion

Advancing gender-inclusive cooperative systems and actively upgrading women into financial and executive leadership roles within rural agricultural governance.

Traceable Value Chains

Developing robust digital supply infrastructures to create completely auditable networks from farm gate to consumer destinations.

Climate-Smart Cultivation

Driving climate-resilient and environmentally conscious agroforestry models that rejuvenate local soil systems and protect biodiversity boundaries.
