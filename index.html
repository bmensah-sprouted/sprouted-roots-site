Transforming Agriculture from the Roots Up

Based in Ghana, Sprouted Roots LBG is a non-governmental organization dedicated to cultivating inclusive and sustainable agricultural value chains.

We act as a critical local strategic partner to ethical chocolate brands, producer cooperatives, and international sustainability initiatives. By fostering strong producer-consumer connections, advancing cooperative systems, and enhancing product quality, we promote market access and environmental integrity.

Our Vision

To be a leading catalyst for sustainable and inclusive value chain development in West Africa, where producers thrive, markets are fair, and agriculture sustains people and the planet.

Our Mission

To strengthen cooperative systems, support ethical sourcing, and build resilient agricultural communities through market linkages, certification, and value chain innovation.
